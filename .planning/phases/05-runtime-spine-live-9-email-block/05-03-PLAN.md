<objective>
Make the real `caprun` binary use the single unified broker dispatch and the typed claims protocol. Delete the duplicate worker-connection dispatch loop and the live SubmitPlanNode placeholder from `main.rs`, point `caprun` at `brokerd::server::run_broker_server`, and upgrade the confined `caprun-worker` to extract claims, report them typed, submit a plan node, and exit non-success on a block. Update the two Linux-gated `e2e.rs` substrate tests so they reflect the new protocol (the old `ReportRead` byte-count actor encoding and 3-event benign chain no longer exist).

Purpose: ASM-01 (no second loop in the binary), ASM-02 (live executor path; placeholder gone), ASM-03 (worker emits typed ReportClaims).
Output: A rewired `main.rs`, a new-protocol `worker.rs`, and updated `e2e.rs` tests that stay green on Linux.
</objective>
