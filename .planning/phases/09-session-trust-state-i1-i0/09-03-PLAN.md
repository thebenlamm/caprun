|----------|-------------|
| worker (confined) -> broker IPC | `ReportClaims`/`SubmitPlanNode` messages cross UDS; session identity and trust state must be broker-resolved, never taken from the message |
| broker -> sessions/events tables | the demotion write crosses into the audit DAG; read-model and ledger must never disagree |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-09-06 | Spoofing / EoP | Worker IPC self-declares session `Active` to bypass Step 0.5 | critical | mitigate | `session_status` sourced exclusively from the broker's per-connection local, passed as `&session_status` into the executor — never from `plan_node`/IPC (DESIGN §4) |
| T-09-07 | Repudiation / Tampering | Demotion partially applied (status updated but no `session_demoted` event, or vice versa) on crash/error | high | mitigate | Atomic same-lock pair inside `mint_from_read` (DESIGN §5, RESEARCH Pitfall 5); no second lock acquisition |
