<objective>
Wire git.push into the broker's control flow as an ALWAYS-confirm-gated sink: the server.rs dispatch makes even a clean/Allowed git.push freeze its new-oid, insert a pending confirmation, and return BlockedPendingConfirmation (never auto-dispatch); confirmation.rs threads the frozen new-oid through the MAC'd snapshot, adds the entry-guard + Step-4.8d `prepare_git_push` precheck + Step-7 dispatch arm (invoking the Plan 44-03 transfer driver), and renders the commit-range + per-file taint-provenance summary at confirm.

Purpose: DESIGN §1.6 (payload-vs-destination anti-TOCTOU: the human authorizes the PAYLOAD, and the pack is frozen from the confirmed range at-or-after confirm) + §1.7 (confirm-release audit-gap discipline, terminal-event-before-terminal-state, P33/P34). Per the locked decision, git.push does NOT adopt github.pr's coarse session-scoped grant — its gate is a per-push confirm that shows and freezes the payload, because the pushed PACK is worker-controlled and never an I2 arg. A clean remote/refspec must NOT bare-auto-dispatch worker-controlled pack content.

Output: A confirm-gated, anti-TOCTOU-frozen, payload-provenance-surfacing git.push dispatch path.
</objective>
